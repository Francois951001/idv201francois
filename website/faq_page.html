Frequently Asked Questions

The top 9 frequently asked questions with answers:

  1. Q: What is Sound Overload Music Festival?
    2. A: Sound Overload Music Festival is a three-day music festival starring various bands performing for specific periods of time.

  2. Q: How can I attend Sound Overload Music Festival?
    3. A: In order to attend Sound Overload Music Festival you must purchase a ticket to the event. See Home for ticket details.

  3. Q: Where is Sound Overload Music Festival?
    4. A: This year's edition of Sound Overload Music Festival will be held on March 27th, 28th, and 29th, 2015.

  4. Q: Where is Sound Overload Music Festival?
    5. A: The 2015 edition of Sound Overload Music Festival will be taking place at the Coca-Cola Dome, Johannesburg. See Contact Us page for direct address.

  5. Q: Is there a minimum age for Sound Overload Music Festival?
    6. A: Yes. Sound Overload Music Festival is for those 18 years of age and older ONLY.

  6. Q: What services does Sound Overload provide at the Festival?
    7. A: Friendly security starff, a clean & safe environment, information booths, on-site ATM's, First Aid / Medical services, free water stations, restroom facilities, cell-phone recharge stations, a lost and found service, and more will be available at this years Sound Overload Music Festival.

  7. Q: Will I be able to purchase food and beverages at the festival?
    8. A: A wide variety of food and beverages will be available all day and night via the various vendors at the festival grounds.

  8. Q: What items can I bring into the festival?
    9. A: All items will be checked at the entrance. Food and Drinks are allowed, no alcohol.

  9. Q: What are the options for acceptable forms of identification upon arrival?
    10. A: Standard Identification Document (ID), Drivers License, and/or Passport.
